Problem

     SVAD students have developed strong creative skills but lack visibility outside the classroom. Without a central platform, many struggle to connect with potential clients and showcase their talent.

Solution

     A professional online hub called "The Maker's Gallery" where SVAD students can showcase their work across creative disciplines. Supported by a fun and engagingpromotional video, the platform connects student artists with real world opportunities.

Why it Works

  • Visibility: A central destination makes it easy for clients to discover emerging talent.
  • Storytelling: The promotional video humanizes the creatives, helping audiences connect with their process and passion.

Execution Plan

Website

  1. Curate a list of students across specialties (photography, film, design, ceramics, etc.)
  2. Build individual profiles with headshots, contact info, and portfolios
  3. Organize work by art medium for easy navigation
  4. Feature a bold homepage with a one-line mission statement and the embedded promotional video

Video

  1. Spotlight four students from different fields
  2. Blend personal interviews with artistic supporting shots of their creative process
  3. Highlight students' passion and creative process through a fun, engaging tone
  4. End each segment with: "You can find me and many other artists on The Maker's Gallery"
